About Dolgormaa Bat

I'm a researcher and strategist based between Mongolia and the UK, with a background spanning consulting, financial services, energy regulation and consumer goods.

I started out in policy research in Ulaanbaatar, moved into advisory work with financial institutions and development partners across Mongolia, and gradually shifted toward the quantitative and data side — completing an MSc in Business Analytics at Lancaster in 2023.

Since then I've worked in regulated energy in the UK — smart metering governance, Ofgem submissions, data platform transformation — and more recently in property analytics in London.

I keep close to where analytical methods and tooling are heading — not because new technology is inherently interesting, but because the gap between what's methodologically possible and what most organisations are actually doing tends to be where the useful work is. I'm more interested in what holds up in practice than what reads well in a deck.
